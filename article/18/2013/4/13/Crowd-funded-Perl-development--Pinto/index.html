Crowd funded Perl development: Pinto

Apr 13, 2013 by David Farrell

Jeffrey Thalhammer, developer of Perl Critic is working on a Pinto, an application for managing stand-alone repositories of Perl modules. He’s seeking crowd funding to continue the development of Pinto. This looks a like a great opportunity to help Jeffrey and support community-driven Perl development.

See brian d foy’s blog post for further detail and how to get involved.
